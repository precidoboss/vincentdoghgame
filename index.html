Grotto L1 — Avalanche — Gaming Platform

Somewhere inside Grotto L1 — a gaming platform on Avalanche where indie games launch, NFTs drop, and $HERESY (only 500 ever, don't ask) fuels the economy — there lives a Discord activity bot.

The bot's job: show which player is doing stuff in the app. Bought a game. Left a review. Joined a jam. Simple.

The devs launched it. The bot fired. Instead of your name, it printed:
Unknown

Not a crash. Not an error message. Just… Unknown. Like the bot looked at you, shrugged, and filed you under "figure it out later."

The community saw it. They laughed. Then they started cheering. "Wow, Unknown is SO active today." And thus — the meme was born.
