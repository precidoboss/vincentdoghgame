Terms of Service

1. Acceptance
By connecting your wallet to the Grotto Pass Bank, you agree to these terms.

2. Service Description
The Pass Bank is a community vault for ERC-1155 game passes on the Grotto L1 blockchain.

3. One Claim Per Wallet
Each wallet address may claim one pass per pass type. This is enforced on-chain.

4. No Guarantees
Pass availability depends on community deposits.

5. Smart Contract Risk
All transactions are final and irreversible.

6. No Financial Advice
Nothing here constitutes financial or investment advice.

Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect
Wallet address, Grotto player ID, pass requested, and request timestamp.

2. Blockchain Data
All on-chain transactions are permanently recorded on Grotto L1.

3. How We Use It
Request data is used solely to process pass distribution.

4. Third-Party Services
