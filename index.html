Terms of Service

1. Acceptance
By connecting your wallet to the Grotto Pass Bank, you agree to these terms. If you do not agree, do not use this service.

2. Service Description
The Pass Bank is a community vault for ERC-1155 game passes on the Grotto L1 blockchain. It allows users to deposit game passes for the community and request one pass per registered type.

3. One Claim Per Wallet
Each wallet address may claim one pass per pass type. This is enforced on-chain and cannot be bypassed. Claims via Grotto session are subject to manual review and sent at the operator's discretion.

4. No Guarantees
Pass availability depends on community deposits. The operator makes no guarantee that passes will be available at any given time. Vault balances are public and visible on-chain.

5. Smart Contract Risk
All transactions are final and irreversible on the blockchain. The operator is not responsible for lost funds due to user error, network issues, or smart contract bugs.

6. No Financial Advice
Nothing on this platform constitutes financial or investment advice. Game passes are digital assets with no guaranteed monetary value.

7. Changes
These terms may be updated at any time. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect
When you submit a pass request via Grotto session, we store your wallet address, Grotto player ID, the pass requested, and request timestamp in our database. No personal information beyond your public wallet address is collected.

2. Blockchain Data
All on-chain transactions (deposits, claims, withdrawals) are permanently recorded on the Grotto L1 blockchain and are publicly visible. We do not control or delete this data.

3. How We Use Your Data
Request data is used solely to process pass distribution. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or profiling purposes.

4. Data Retention
Request records are kept indefinitely for audit and anti-spam purposes. You may contact us to request deletion of off-chain records associated with your wallet.

5. Third-Party Services
This service uses Supabase for database storage and the Grotto API for identity verification. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Contact
For privacy inquiries, reach out through the Grotto community channels.
