⬡ INTERACTIVE GUIDE — HOW MOATS WORK
1 / 8
Your edge in the Moats ecosystem
MOATS INTEL tracks all 11 moats live — staked/locked/burned positions, token prices, reward history, and council scores. Use the Investment Allocator to plan your USDC into optimal token positions, and the Signals tab to spot which moats have the most upside.
⬡ Moats Protocol
A Moat is a tokenised vault. You deposit a moat's token using Stake (1×), Lock (2.04–5×), or Burn (10×) and earn Moat Points. Points are siloed per moat. Participating in multiple moats unlocks a Diversity Multiplier on your Council Score.
🏰 Score System
Council Score
Total Points × Diversity Multiplier (1.0×→1.5×). Governs voting weight and ranking.
Fort Score
Sum of all Moat Points. Your % of ecosystem total = your airdrop share.

Features at a Glance
🏦 My Positions
Connect your wallet to see all your moat positions on-chain — staked, locked, burned amounts, active locks with countdowns, and pending rewards. Click any moat to manage it: swap tokens, stake, lock, burn, unstake, claim rewards. All moats shown even if you haven't joined.
📋 Copy Moat
Mirror a top wallet's strategy with your own funds. Pick a wallet from the leaderboard, choose your budget (USDC or AVAX) and method (stake/lock/burn), and execute in one click. Smart routing via Pharaoh · LFJ V1 · Odos · Paraswap — finds the best swap price automatically.
💰 Invest
Enter your USDC budget and goal. The allocator picks moats, calculates token amounts to buy, and projects your points, rank, and ecosystem share. Auto Agent mode picks everything automatically.
🔬 Signals
12 auto-generated signals: whale concentration, low eco share, diversity bonus opportunities, price momentum, single-wallet dominance, LP liquidity warnings, hottest 7d activity, and more. Updated on every data refresh.
⬡ Moats
Tap any moat card for full detail — Overview (on-chain staked/locked/burned), Token & Price (live from DexScreener + GeckoTerminal), Leaderboard with usernames, and Rewards history.
🔎 Wallet
Analyze any wallet: Council Score, Fort Score, per-moat rank, ecosystem share %, and personalized strategy signals. Enter any 0x address.
📡 Activity
Live event stream with type, moat, and time filters. Shows real token amounts from on-chain data. Filter by staked/locked/burned/rewards/unstake.
💧 hCASH Moat
Special LP moat — pairs AVAX + hCASH on Pharaoh Exchange to get Volatile LP tokens, then stakes them. Use My Positions → hCASH Moat to add liquidity and manage your LP position directly from this app. Burn is disabled for this moat.
